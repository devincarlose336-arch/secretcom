# Secretcom - User Manual

## Getting Started

### Registration
1. Open the Secretcom app
2. Tap "New user? Register with Meeting ID"
3. Enter your name
4. Enter your assigned Meeting ID (format: SC-XXXXXXXX)
5. Tap "REGISTER"

### Login
1. Open the app
2. Enter your username and password
3. Tap "LOGIN"

## Joining a Room

1. After login, you'll see the room list
2. Your Meeting ID is displayed at the top
3. Tap "JOIN" on any available room (max 25 participants)
4. Full rooms show "FULL" and cannot be joined

## Push-to-Talk Communication

1. In a room, you'll see the participant list
2. Press and hold the green microphone button to speak
3. Release the button to stop speaking
4. A green indicator shows when someone is speaking
5. Your audio is transmitted to all participants in the room

## Understanding the Interface

### Room List Screen
- Shows all 4 rooms with participant counts
- Progress bar indicates room capacity
- Refresh button updates room status
- Logout button exits the app

### Audio Room Screen
- Top bar: Room name and participant count
- Participant list: Shows all users with speaking/muted indicators
- Push-to-Talk button: Hold to speak, release to stop
- Exit Room button: Returns to room list

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| Cannot connect | Check internet connection |
| No audio | Ensure microphone permission is granted |
| Audio quality poor | Move to better network coverage |
| Cannot join room | Room may be full (25 max) |
| App crashes | Update to latest version |

## Tips
- Grant all requested permissions for best experience
- Use headphones to avoid echo
- Speak clearly and close to the microphone
- Exit rooms when not in use
